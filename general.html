General Dermatology

Cabin 101, 1st floor

Head of Department: MUDr. Jana Nováková

Team: MUDr. Tomáš Beneš, MUDr. Eva Horáková, MUDr. Martin Doležal

The General Dermatology Department covers all common and chronic skin diseases, offering comprehensive diagnostics, prevention, and treatment for patients of all ages.

Overview:

Services:

- Diagnosis and management of acne, eczema, psoriasis, fungal and bacterial infections

- Preventive full-body skin checks and mole mapping

- Phototherapy, cryotherapy, and chemical peels

- Individual skincare and treatment plans

Equipment:

Digital dermatoscopes, UV phototherapy cabin, and advanced imaging technology.

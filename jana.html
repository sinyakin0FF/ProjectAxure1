Dermatology is one of the few fields where early visual diagnosis can save lives — especially in melanoma cases. The skin reflects the state of our internal organs, so dermatologists often detect internal diseases first. Modern dermato-oncology combines medical, surgical, and aesthetic approaches to preserve both health and appearance.

Professional focus

Dr. Nováková focuses on aesthetic dermatology, anti-aging treatments, and skin health management. She combines medical dermatology with cosmetic procedures, including laser therapy, skin rejuvenation, and treatment of scars and pigmentation. Her goal is to provide personalized care that balances health, function, and appearance.

About

Dr. Jana Nováková is passionate about helping her patients feel confident in their skin. She emphasizes prevention and patient education, believing that knowledge about skin health is as important as treatment itself. Known for her empathy and attention to detail, she maintains a holistic approach, considering lifestyle, nutrition, and mental well-being alongside medical treatment.

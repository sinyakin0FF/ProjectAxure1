Dermatologic Surgery

Cabin 401, 4th floor – Surgical Unit

Head of Department: MUDr. Michal Kratochvíl

Team: MUDr. Roman Hruška, MUDr. Daniel Vacek, MUDr. Jana Pokorná

The Dermatologic Surgery Department provides both medical and cosmetic surgical treatments, focusing on safety, precision, and minimal scarring.

Overview:

Services:

- Surgical and laser removal of moles, cysts, and tumors

- Skin biopsies and histological diagnostics

- Cosmetic suturing and scar revision

- Postoperative care and monitoring

Special features:

All procedures performed in sterile conditions by experienced dermatologic surgeons using state-of-the-art equipment.

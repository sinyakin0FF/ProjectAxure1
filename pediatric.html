Pediatric Dermatology

Cabin 202, 2nd floor

Head of Department: MUDr. Klára Dvořáková

Team: MUDr. Veronika Kučerová, MUDr. Petr Novotný, MUDr. Alena Černá

The Pediatric Dermatology Department provides specialized care for children with skin diseases — from birth to adolescence. It focuses on early detection, safe treatment, and prevention.

Overview:

Services:

- Treatment of eczema, warts, hemangiomas, and genetic skin disorders

- Allergy and sensitivity testing for children

- Parental counseling on daily care and prevention

- Collaboration with pediatricians and allergologists

Approach:

Gentle, child-friendly environment ensuring minimal stress for young patients.

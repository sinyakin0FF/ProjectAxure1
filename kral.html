Psoriasis affects more than 2% of the Czech population and is now considered a systemic inflammatory disease, not just a skin disorder. Recent studies show that untreated psoriasis can increase cardiovascular risk — highlighting the importance of dermatologists in holistic medicine. The introduction of biologic therapy has revolutionized treatment, turning severe cases into fully manageable conditions. Dermatology, therefore, bridges immunology, psychology, and internal medicine more than ever before.

Professional focus

Dr. Král’s expertise includes autoimmune and chronic inflammatory skin diseases such as psoriasis, eczema, and atopic dermatitis. He specializes in the use of biologic and targeted therapies, patient lifestyle coaching, and long-term disease management. His goal is not only to treat visible symptoms but to help patients regain confidence and improve mental well-being.

About

Dr. Jan Král is a compassionate clinician known for his evidence-based approach and deep empathy toward his patients. With over a decade of practice in both hospital and private settings, he emphasizes the connection between mind, immunity, and skin health. Outside of medicine, he’s an avid cyclist and advocates for work-life balance to prevent physician burnout.

About us

Welcome to DerHealth,

At DerHealth, we are dedicated to providing exceptional dermatological care tailored to each patient’s unique needs. Our mission is to help you achieve healthy, radiant skin while ensuring the highest standards of medical excellence and comfort.

Founded in 2010 by Dr. Anna Petrova, a board-certified dermatologist with over 15 years of experience, DerHealth began as a small practice with a vision: to combine advanced medical treatments with compassionate, personalized care. What started as a two-room office has grown into a full-service dermatology center, serving thousands of patients annually, equipped with state-of-the-art technology and a multidisciplinary team of specialists.

Our story

Our mission is simple:

Empower patients with knowledge and care to maintain healthy skin.

Deliver innovative treatments based on the latest research in dermatology.

Create a comfortable and welcoming environment where patients feel heard and valued.

Our Mission

We believe that dermatology is not just about treating skin conditions but enhancing the confidence and well-being of every patient. Our approach combines medical expertise, advanced technology, and a commitment to holistic care. We treat every patient as a partner in their journey toward optimal skin health.

Our Philosophy

Our team of experienced dermatologists, aesthetic specialists, and skincare professionals is dedicated to providing personalized care for a wide range of conditions, including:

 - Acne and rosacea

 - Eczema and psoriasis

 - Skin cancer screenings

 - Cosmetic dermatology

 - Hair and nail disorders

Every team member undergoes regular training and certification to stay at the forefront of dermatology advancements.

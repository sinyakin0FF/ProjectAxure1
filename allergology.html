Allergology

Cabin 204, 2nd floor

Head of Department: MUDr. David Hájek

Team: MUDr. Zuzana Pospíšilová, MUDr. Filip Kopecký, MUDr. Kateřina Bláhová

The Allergology Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of allergic skin conditions. It works closely with immunologists and pulmonologists for holistic care.

Overview:

Services:

- Allergy testing (patch, prick, and blood tests)

- Treatment for urticaria, eczema, and contact dermatitis

- Immunotherapy and desensitization

- Long-term allergy management and prevention

Focus:

Aiming to identify triggers and provide sustainable relief, not just temporary symptom control.
